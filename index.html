DICE FACES
⚔️ Axe — Melee attack. Cancelled by opponent's 🪖 Helms.
🪖 Helm — Melee block. Cancels one enemy ⚔️ Axe.
🏹 Arrow — Ranged attack. Cancelled by opponent's 🛡️ Shields.
🛡️ Shield — Ranged block. Cancels one enemy 🏹 Arrow.
⬡ Blue dot — This die also earns a Token when rolled.
COMBAT RULES
🟡 Health Stones — Lose one per point of damage taken. Reach 0 and you lose.
⬡ Tokens — Earned from dice with blue dots. Spent to activate your God Favor.
⚡ Piercing damage — Ignores all blocks. Goes straight to health.
🔒 Lock Dice — Finalises your roll. Both players lock before favors are revealed.
⏭ Skip — Forfeit your God Favor for this round. Tokens are kept for later.
HOW A ROUND WORKS
1. Roll — Each player rolls 6 dice, keeps chosen ones, rerolls the rest (max 2 rerolls). Click a die to toggle keep/reroll.
2. Lock — Hit "Lock Dice" when happy. Both players lock independently.
3. God Favor — Both players secretly choose a favor level to activate (or skip). Choices reveal simultaneously.
4. Resolve — Damage is calculated: Axes vs Helms, Arrows vs Shields. Unblocked hits deal 1 damage each. Favour effects then apply.
